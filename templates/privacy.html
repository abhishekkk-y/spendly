Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your email address and hashed password when you register.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter into the app.
  • Usage data: basic server logs such as page visits and error events, used solely for maintaining the service.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what is needed to run the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Diagnose technical issues and maintain service reliability.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside of running Spendly.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on servers located within the region where the app is hosted. We apply reasonable security measures — including hashed passwords and access controls — to protect your information from unauthorized access. No method of storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts: loads typefaces from Google's CDN. Google may collect request metadata per their own privacy policy.

We do not integrate analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media trackers. No third party receives your expense data.

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app.
